Tattoo FAQ

How do I book a tattoo?

Use the booking section below and include reference images, size, placement, and preferred dates.

How is pricing determined?

Pricing depends on size, complexity, placement, and time required. A quote is provided after reviewing your request.

Do tattoos hurt?

Discomfort varies by placement and individual tolerance. Most clients describe it as manageable.

How should I prepare?

Eat beforehand, stay hydrated, and avoid alcohol 24 hours before your appointment.

What is the aftercare process?

Aftercare instructions are provided at your appointment. Proper healing is essential for best results.
